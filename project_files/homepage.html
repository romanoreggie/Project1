DEADLIEST CATCH

Fish if you dare...


Instructions

The purpose of this game is to eat as many fish as possible, but be careful which ones you eat.

Puffer Fish will deduct 2x more points than you gain from any other fish you eat.

Use the right and left arrow keys to navigate your shark along the bottom of the screen.

When you are ready to begin, select your difficulty level above.

At anytime you can view these instructions again by clicking the Home button.
